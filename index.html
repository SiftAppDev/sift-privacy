Sift: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

At Sift Labs, we believe your financial life is your business. Sift is a Personal Finance Management (PFM) tool built with "Privacy by Design." It is a Local-First application, meaning your sensitive financial data never leaves your device.

1. Data Processing and "Local-First" Architecture

Sift does not operate a central database or cloud storage. We do not collect, store, or see your budget data. Your information is your own.

  • Financial Data: All income, expenses, and plans are stored in an encrypted local database on your device. We have no access to this data and it is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • No Account Requirement: You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or link a phone number to use Sift.

2. Information Processed by Third Parties

To provide core services, Sift utilizes industry-standard Google Play services for transaction processing and app integrity.

  • Purchase History: When you upgrade to Sift Pro, Google Play manages the transaction. We receive only the necessary verification (transaction ID and status) to unlock your features. This is processed under the basis of Contractual Necessity.
  • App Integrity: We use standard security signals to ensure you are using an official, untampered version of Sift, protecting your device from unauthorized software.

3. Global Data Subject Rights

We respect international privacy standards. Regardless of your location, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your budget data directly within the application interface at any time.
  • Right to Portability: You can export your financial data to CSV format using the built-in export tools.
  • Right to Erasure: You maintain absolute control over the deletion of your local data.

4. Data Deletion

We do not retain your financial data on any servers. You can permanently delete 100% of your data by:

  • Clearing App Storage: Navigate to your device settings to wipe the local database.
  • Uninstallation: Deleting the app removes all local records and data stores.
  • Purchase Management: To manage or delete your purchase history, visit your Google Account Center.

5. Children’s Privacy

Sift is designed for a general adult audience (18+). We do not knowingly process data from individuals under the age of 13.
