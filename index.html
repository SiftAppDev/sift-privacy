Sift: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

At Sift Labs, we believe your financial life is your business. Sift is a Personal Finance Management (PFM) tool built with "Privacy by Design." It is a Local-First application, meaning your sensitive financial data never leaves your device.

1. Data Processing and "Local-First" Architecture

Sift does not operate a central database or cloud storage. We do not collect, store, or see your budget data.

  • Financial Data: All income, expenses, and plans are stored in an encrypted local database on your device (SQLDelight). We have no access to this data.
  • No Account Requirement: You do not need to create an account, provide an email, or link a phone number to use the core features of Sift.

2. Information We Collect (Google Play Billing)

The only data "collected" by Sift is related to premium feature verification through the Google Play Store.

  • Purchase History: When you upgrade to Sift Pro, Google Play provides us with a transaction ID and status to unlock your features. This is processed under the legal basis of Contractual Necessity (to provide the service you paid for).
  • Security & Fraud: Standard Google Play Integrity signals are used to ensure the app is a legitimate version, protecting your device from tampered software.

3. Your Legal Rights (GDPR & Law 133/2011)

Whether you are in the EU or Moldova, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: You can view all your budget data directly in the app.
  • Right to Portability: You can export your data to CSV format at any time using the "Export" feature.
  • Right to Erasure: You have absolute control over your data deletion (see below).

4. Data Deletion and Retention

We do not retain your financial data on any servers. You can delete 100% of your data instantly by:

  • Clearing App Data: Go to Android Settings > Apps > Sift > Storage > Clear Data.
  • Uninstalling: Deleting the app removes the local database and all records.
  • Purchase Records: To manage or delete your purchase history, please visit your Google Account Payments Center.

5. Children’s Privacy

Sift is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under the age of 13.
