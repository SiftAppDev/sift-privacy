Sift: Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

At Sift Labs, your privacy is our foundation. Sift is designed as a "Local-First" application, meaning your financial data belongs to you and stays on your device.

1. Data Collection

Sift does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or financial information to external servers.

  • Financial Data: All income, expenses, and calibration settings are stored locally on your device's internal database.
  • Personal Information: We do not require accounts, email addresses, or phone numbers to use Sift.

2. Third-Party Services

Sift uses standard Google Play libraries for security and billing. We do not share your private financial logs with these or any other third parties.

  • Google Play Billing: Transactions are handled entirely by Google. Sift does not see or store your credit card details.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to it. To delete your data, simply uninstall the app or clear the app's cache in your phone settings.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us at: getsift.app@gmail.com
